Yoga is a perfect practice for beginners seeking to build strength, flexibility, and mental clarity. A daily routine doesn't require much time or experience.

**Why Start with Yoga**

Yoga combines physical movement with breathing techniques and mindfulness. It's accessible, sustainable, and beneficial for both body and mind.

**Simple 10-Minute Daily Routine**

Start with Child's Pose for calm breathing, then move to Cat-Cow stretches. Follow with Downward Dog, hold for several breaths. Finish with a seated forward fold to relax.

**Building Consistency**

Practice at the same time each day. Even 10 minutes daily beats occasional longer sessions. Your body adapts quickly, and you'll notice improvements in flexibility within weeks.

**Listen to Your Body**

Never push into pain. Yoga is about progress, not perfection. With consistent practice, your flexibility and strength will improve naturally.
