Beginning a yoga practice can feel overwhelming, but this 7-day program breaks it down into manageable daily sessions.

## Day 1: Breathing & Centering
Start with pranayama (breathing exercises) and basic meditation.

## Day 2: Standing Poses
Focus on Mountain Pose, Warrior I, and Downward-Facing Dog.

## Day 3-7: Progressive Practice
Each day builds on the previous, introducing new poses and deeper stretches.

Consistency is more important than perfection. Start today!
